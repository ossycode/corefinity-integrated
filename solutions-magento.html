Platform Overview

Magento offers unmatched flexibility and scalability but that power comes with complexity. Large catalogues, intricate integrations and peak sales periods can overwhelm standard hosting. Corefinity’s platform is purpose built for Magento, delivering the performance, security and stability needed to keep stores fast and revenue flowing, no matter the traffic or customisation.

With Corefinity, you get:

  • Consistent checkout performance during promotions and peaks

  • A support team that have been working with Magento since the early days

  • Safe releases with staging, rollbacks, and zero‑downtime deploys for updates

We optimise every Magento environment for catalogue size, extension load, and checkout flow. Caching, database tuning, and queue workers keep pages fast and responsive—even during heavy traffic or large-scale promotions.

  • 0 Downtime deployments

    The stack offers a built-in 0-deployment pipeline that is customisable to your requirements and work flows with functionality such as auto-deployments to staging environments and easy rollbacks.

    Log management

    We’ve taken log management to another level by implementing best of the open source with our platform agnostic control panel allowing you to view and manage logs of multiple servers and services in place.

    Full automation around a range of trusted cloud providers allows flexibility around how much your infrastructure is hated where, helping you meet your disaster recovery requirements.

    Corefinity partners with Let's Encrypt to provide universal, fully automated SSL certificates across it’s infrastructure. We also have options for custom SSLs as well as Extensive Validation SSls that can be purchased.

  • Ramdisk

    Our software stack uniquely reduces I/O and HDD operations drastically by placing all of the Magento and your custom/plugins codebase on a RAM disk.

    Snapshots

    Automated, scheduled and high-frequency snapshots managed in one place with multiple options on how to recover them. From recovery in place to creating a new server or even a new URL for your application, we’ve got them all.

  • Performance focused
